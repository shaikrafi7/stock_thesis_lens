# Product Vision

## Core Idea
A personal AI investment partner that helps users answer:

> "Is my investment thesis still valid?"

---

## MVP Promise
In 10 seconds, a user can:
- See portfolio health
- See which stocks need attention
- Understand why

---

## Target User
- Serious long-term retail investors
- Not day traders
- Not passive index-only investors

---

## Problem
Investors:
- Forget why they bought a stock
- React emotionally to price
- Get overwhelmed by news
- Lack structured thinking

---

## Solution
A system that:
- Captures investment thesis
- Monitors it continuously
- Alerts when it weakens or breaks
- Explains why clearly

---

## Non-Goals (MVP)
- No brokerage integration
- No real-time trading signals
- No complex social sentiment tracking
- No full AI autopilot

---

## Long-Term Vision
A true personal AI investment partner that:
- Understands user psychology
- Tracks thinking over time
- Guides decisions with context
- Adapts to market conditions
